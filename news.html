Hero of the Trojan War | 5.3 PC Update Notes

Meet new legendary warrior of Greece- Achilles, Hero of the Trojan War! Achilles adapts to the tide of Battle and doing in right best way.

ABILITIES

Passive: Gift of the Gods Achilles adapts to the tide of Battle. Achilles can choose to wear armor, granting him bonus Health and Protections, or forgo it, granting him bonus Movement Speed and Physical Power.
Armored Health Bonus: 35 +10 per Level
Armored Protections Bonus: 5 +1 per Level
OR
Unarmored Movement Speed Bonus: 3% +.25% per Level
Unarmored Physical Power Bonus: 10 +2 per Level

    Active:
  1. Shield of Achilles
    Achilles punches forward with the edge of his Shield, inflicting massive damage and stunning enemy targets hit by the impact. The force of his punch continues to radiate past his initial target area, dealing 80% damage to targets farther away.
    Damage: 100/160/220/280/340 (90% of your Physical Power)
    Stun Duration: 1.1/1.2/1.3/1.4/1.5s
  2. Radiant Glory
    Achilles is blessed by the gods, giving him bonus Physical Power, Protections, and Crowd Control Reduction for 6 seconds. While this blessing is active, Achilles will heal himself upon successfully damaging enemies with abilities.
    Heal: 23/26/29/32/35 (10% of your Physical Power)
    Max Heals per Ability: 2/2/3/3/4
    Physical Power: +15%
    Protections: +15%
    Crowd Control Reduction: 20%
  3. Combat Dodge
    Achilles dodges his enemies' attacks before striking them in swift response. If Achilles successfully hits an enemy god with this strike, Achilles can use this ability once more before it goes on Cooldown.
    Damage: 65/105/145/185/225 (50% of your Physical Power)
  4. Fatal Strike
    Achilles winds up a large attack and dashes forward. While dashing, Achilles will pass through minions, stop and hit the first enemy god he encounters, dealing damage to all he hits. If the god is below 30% Health they are Executed. If Achilles successfully Executes a god with this ability, he can use it again, up to 5 times. As Achilles successfully Executes his enemies, he becomes more reckless in combat and leaves his heel exposed. Achilles will become more susceptible to damage, stacking up to 5 times.
    Damage: 180/270/360/450/540 (100% of your Physical Power)
    Execute Threshold: 30%
    Damage Taken Increase: 10%

BUG FIXES

Fixed an issue where god selection icons would take a long time to load when first entering a match lobby.
Fixed an issue where Ah Muzen Cab’s Passive Ability was described as a “Pet” Ability.
Fixed an issue where Poseidon’s Whirlpool had an incorrect “Tide” number.
Fixed an issue where The Morrigan’s icon would be revealed on the minimap even when stealthed.
Fixed an issue where Discordia’s Passive Ability Buff FX could become invisible.
Fixed an issue where Cu Chulainn would be healed for an incorrect amount from Meditation Cloak.
Fixed an issue in Da Ji’s Adventure where a Summoner could spawn out of reach of most player attacks.
Fixed an issue where DJ Ohm’s VXG isn’t audible to enemy team.
Fixed an issue with Hades ultimate playing Ragnarok voice lines on other skins.
Fixed an issue when Nike ults on Ullr, her laurel shows up around his neck.
Fixed an issue where Gods page ability icons disappear after you highlight them.
Fixed an issue with Arachne’s third attack having a cleave.
Fixed an issue where if Isis ulted on top of the Fire Giant, the ult automatically exploded.
Fixed an issue with Evolved Assassin’s Blessing returning 25 mana instead of 20.
Fixed an issue with Shader Quality automatically being set to ‘low’ in video options.
Fixed an issue where closing the client or getting pulled into a queue with 3-choice chest open and unpicked leaves you in state that crashes client on relog.
Fixed an issue with missing textures on Apollo’s Winner’s Circle Car.
Fixed an issue with Pyromancer death global audio not being global.
Fixed an issue where resolution scale is automatically set to 0% upon opening the options menu.
